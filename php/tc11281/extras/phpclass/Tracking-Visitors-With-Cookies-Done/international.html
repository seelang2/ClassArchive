Russia Tests US Spork Dominance

Launch in the Barents Sea Pose Threat to US Sporks

Moscow, Russia: Russian scientists claim they are hot on the tail of America with their own improved version of the spork, the Skadle. The Skadle is over ten times more powerful than the spork, as it combines a ladle and a skewer. Although American scientists acknowledge the power of the Skadle, they claim it will never catch on because the name is just not catchy enough.
