Current Examination Committee

The Examination Committee is composed of Board appointed ABOMS Diplomates. These members are selected to serve in leadership roles through the development and administration for both the ABOMS computer-based and oral certifying examinations. The ABOMS Examiners exude the highest level of professionalism and exceptional knowledge in the specialty.
